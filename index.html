📋 Rules & Regulations

  1. Keep it study-related. Only post reviewers, notes, summaries, digests, or links to real course material.
  2. No profanity, slurs, or hate speech. Posts with offensive or discriminatory language will be removed.
  3. No personal attacks or harassment. Don't call out, insult, or target any student, teacher, or staff member.
  4. No spam, ads, or unrelated links. Keep posts focused on academics — no promotions or junk content.
  5. Give credit where it's due. Don't claim someone else's notes as entirely your own.
  6. Attachment links must be safe and relevant. No phishing links, malware, or content unrelated to your notes.
  7. No duplicate posts. Check the shelf before posting — the system will also flag likely duplicates.
  8. Everything here is public. Anything you post is visible to every classmate who opens this page.
  9. Violations get removed. Posts that break these rules will be taken down without notice.
